About TMRT

TMRT (The Marine Robotics Team) is a dynamic and multidisciplinary team of passionate engineers, researchers, and innovators from K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering (KJSCE). United by a common vision of pushing the boundaries of underwater technology, TMRT specializes in the design, development, and deployment of Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) for real-world applications and international competitions.

Our team brings together bright minds from mechanical, electronics, software, and marketing domains, fostering collaboration across disciplines to create cutting-edge underwater robotic systems. From concept and simulation to fabrication and testing, every aspect of our vehicle is handled entirely in-house, enabling us to apply theoretical knowledge to practical, hands-on innovation.

We proudly represent KJSCE in prestigious competitions such as SAUVC (Singapore AUV Challenge) and NIOT-SAVe, where we compete against top institutes and demonstrate our expertise in autonomy, computer vision, acoustics, and control systems. Beyond competitions, our mission is to cultivate a culture of technological curiosity and interdisciplinary excellence.
